How the Game Works

Click the blade to gain clicks. Clicks give coins and fragments. Coins can be exchanged for coupons on the market page. Fragments unlock upgrades later. Daily challenges give extra rewards.

Aetherfall Archive

The Aetherfall blade was discovered in a research facility. Repeated strikes cause the blade to release energy. That energy can be converted into fragments and coins. The collected resources can be exchanged for rewards in the market.
