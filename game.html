THE ARCHIVES

The World of Nexus

Nexus was once a paradise of light until the "Aetherfall" occurred, shattering the sky into billions of pieces. These pieces are the Fragments you collect. Only the Weaver—the one who holds the Great Blade—can gather them to restore the world.

The Mission

Your goal is simple: Click to generate energy, Level up to increase your capacity, and use your Fragments to buy artifacts that increase your power. Once you reach Level 100, you will be strong enough to face the final chapter.
