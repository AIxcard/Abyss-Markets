Lore

Fragments were discovered decades ago during a deep seismic scan beneath an abandoned research facility. At first they appeared to be simple crystalline structures, but scientists quickly noticed that the material emitted enormous energy pulses when struck with repeated force.

The phenomenon became known as resonance amplification. Each strike caused the fragments to vibrate faster and release more energy. Entire laboratories were built around this discovery, and eventually the technology powered floating infrastructure and advanced weaponry.

However, the fragments were not native to Earth. Geological scans revealed that they originated from microscopic dimensional fractures deep beneath the crust. When the first large-scale extraction occurred, those fractures widened and triggered the Resonance Collapse.

Cities powered by fragment reactors began to fail as energy feedback loops destabilized their systems. Entire regions were lost overnight. The world shifted from scientific exploration to survival and containment.

Now fragments are rare and heavily traded. Markets like the Abyssal Exchange deal in fragment shards, relic upgrades, and forbidden technology. Those capable of harvesting fragments have become powerful figures in the new world.

Guide

Aetherfall is a clicker game built around fragment energy and resonance combos. Each click represents a strike that resonates with the fragment core, generating energy in the form of clicks and coins.

Combos increase when you click repeatedly without pausing for more than three seconds. Higher combos increase the number of clicks gained per strike, allowing you to progress much faster.

Coins are used in the shop to purchase upgrades, fragments, and other enhancements. Fragments themselves are a rarer resource used for stronger upgrades that permanently increase your power.

Story missions guide progression and reward coins and experience points. Daily missions refresh every 24 hours and provide reliable rewards if completed.

Milestones unlock every five levels and reward large coin or fragment bonuses. These milestones go all the way to level 100.
