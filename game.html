Nexus Master Guide

1. The Economy

  • Gold (0.5% drop): Earned randomly while clicking. Used for Coupon purchases and the Nexus Forge.
  • Fragments (0.1% drop): Extremely rare. These are used for permanent Artifacts in the Fragment Shop.

2. Progression

  • Combos: Clicking rapidly builds a combo. If you stop for 2.5 seconds, it resets to zero!
  • Chapters: Chapter 1 has 10 distinct parts. Each part increases in difficulty but gives better EXP and Gold.
  • Nexus Forge: This is where you combine Gold and Fragments to get the biggest Power boosts in the game.

3. Leveling

Each level requires more EXP. Max level is currently 100. Reach milestones (Level 5, 10, etc.) to claim massive Fragment bonuses in the Milestones menu.
