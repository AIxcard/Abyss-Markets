Game Guide

Clicking

Click the sword to generate clicks. Each click produces power based on your upgrades and combo multiplier.

Combo System

Continuous clicking increases combo. Higher combos increase click power and fragment drop chances.

Fragments

Fragments are rare materials generated through combos. They are used to buy permanent upgrades.

Upgrades

Upgrades permanently increase your clicking strength, automate clicks, or improve fragment generation.

Coupons

Coupons can be purchased using coins and redeemed within the Abyssal Market.

Milestones

Every 5 levels unlocks milestone rewards containing coins and fragments.
