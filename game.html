Survival Guide

The Combo Engine

Every click adds to your combo. If you stop for more than 3 seconds, your combo resets. High combos are the only way to effectively farm fragments.

Aether Fragments

These rare drops occur randomly but scale with your combo. Use them in the Fragment Shop for permanent power increases.

Market Coupons

Coupons are limited stock items that reset every week. They allow you to purchase high-tier items at the Abyssal Market for a fraction of the cost.
